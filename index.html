Lot View Angle of South Manhattan (A Pluto derived map)

Analysis Summary
The viewing angle of a building from street can be seen as an approximate landscaping measure showing how much a building can be felt as vertical view blocker. Two required parameters of planar length (d) and height of each lot are derived from Pluto dataset.

$$d = sqrt{LotArea}.$$
$$h = 10 * (num of floors)$$
